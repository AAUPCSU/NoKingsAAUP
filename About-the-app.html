NoKingsAAUP is the official mobile phone app for the No Kings 3 rally on March 28, 2026, in Fort Collins, Colorado. This app has been designed by local volunteer scientists and artists from the American Association of University Professors at CSU to provide a fun and safe way to help our community to build solidarity, take action, and organize for sustained progressive change.

Key Features

INFORMATION ABOUT LOCAL ORGANIZATIONS

  • Learn about local groups and progressive activities
  • Access real-time updates on rally information, location, schedule, chants and songs
  • Meet other progressive members of our community who share your values and who are working for local change

PLAY AAUP PARTNER BINGO

  • Visit and engage with tables and partner organizations at the rally
  • Learn how local organizations are fighting for social justice
  • Scan QR codes to complete a bingo card
  • Unlock fish avatars to demonstrate your support for progressive causes

CREATE A FISH AVATAR

  • Design a unique fish avatar with custom shapes and colors
  • Write a message for your avatar to share with the community
  • Join the "reFISHtance" against adversaries in the federal government who threaten our communities

BUILD COMMUNITY SOLIDARITY

  • Watch as more fish join the animated resistance
  • Work with the community to distribute power and strengthen the resistance against key threats
  • Like and support messages from other participants
  • See your impact as together we tackle injustice

STAY CONNECTED

  • Share QR codes to invite friends to join
  • Access real-time updates on rally information, location, and schedule
  • Connect with partner organizations and resources
  • View signs from the rally

COMMUNITY MODERATION

  • Block users you don't want to see
  • Report inappropriate content
  • Engage within a safe space for political expression

100% LOCAL CONTENT

  • All app content has been created and curated by Fort Collins community members
  • All artwork in the app was hand-drawn by local artists
  • All community action tables referenced in the app are local to Fort Collins
  • All signs displayed in the app will have been created by local community members

Privacy and Data Safety

PRIVACY POLICY

  • You have complete control over what data you share. The app only collects information that you intentionally provide, and you can delete it at any time.
  • The app does not track your location.
  • The app does not collect or share photos, audio or videos.

ONLINE AND OFFLINE SAFETY

  • The app allows you to verify QR codes befoore following any links.
  • The app can be used fully offline allowing you to save rally information for latr use at home without connecting to the Internet.
  • The app gives you the power to block users and report inappropriate content.
