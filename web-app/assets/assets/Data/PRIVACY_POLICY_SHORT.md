# Privacy Policy Summary

Below is a summary of our privacy policy for the No Kings app. For the full details, please visit the complete privacy policy linked at the bottom.

Note -- you control what information you share. You can use the app completely anonymously if you choose, and you can play Bingo in Airplane mode, so that you do not have to connect to the internet.  We only collect the information you provide, and we take your privacy seriously.

## What We Collect

**When you sign up (optional):**
- User name for your fish avatar
- Protest message to amplify (e.g., "Protect Funding for Science")
- Email address if you want updates on AAUP-CSU events

**When you play:**
- Your bingo game progress and score
- Fish avatar color and shape you select
- Which messages you "like" (show support for)
- Timestamp of when you sign up or update info

**Permissions Needed:**
- **Camera permission** is used only for QR code scanning — no photos are collected, stored, or sent

**Anonymous:**
- You can use the app without signing up. A random ID is created just for your game progress.

## Email Privacy Protection

**Important:** When you submit your signup information:
1. Your email is sent to our database for AAUP-CSU to contact you about events
2. **After submission**, the actual email is immediately deleted from your device and replaced with a masked version (e.g., "b****")
3. The masked email prevents accidental re-submission of your actual email on future updates
4. If you want to provide a different email address, simply clear the field and enter a new valid email

This two-step process protects your email in the cache while still allowing AAUP-CSU to contact you.

## What We DON'T Collect

❌ We do **NOT** collect any personal data you don't provide  
❌ We do **NOT** sell, trade, or rent your information  
❌ We do **NOT** take photos or record audio or video 
❌ We do **NOT** track your location  
❌ We do **NOT** collect browsing history  

## How We Use Your Data

✓ Game functionality and progress tracking  
✓ Showing your fish and message in the Resistance School montage  
✓ Contacting you about AAUP-CSU events (if you provide an email)  
✓ Displaying community engagement (if you "like" messages)  

## Data Sharing

- **Email:** Shared only with AAUP-CSU for the purpose of contacting you about future AAUP-CSU events
- **Messages & Fish:** Visible to all app users in the montage
- **Everything else:** Kept private to you

## Your Control

You can:
- Update or remove your information at anytime
- Delete your email at any time by entering a new valid email or leaving it blank
- Unlike messages at any time
- Use the app completely anonymously
- Remove your "like" from any message
- Block or report inappropriate messages

## Storage & Security

- Your data is stored securely using Firebase Firestore (Google's cloud database)
- Some information (game state, avatar preferences) is stored on your device
- Actual email addresses are masked in local storage for your protection
- Clearing the app will remove all local data
